Stop losing revenue from cookieless traffic.

The digital landscape is evolving, and with privacy becoming a top priority, more and more users are browsing without cookies. With RevenuX, you won’t miss out on valuable ad revenue from this cookieless traffic. Our proprietary algorithms ensure that your ads continue to perform at their best, even when user data is unavailable, keeping your income steady despite privacy restrictions.

Boost your bids with Prebid.js enrichment.

RevenuX takes your header bidding strategy to new heights by enriching bid requests within Prebid.js. This means you can unlock higher bids and greater competition among advertisers, leading to increased revenue. Best of all, there’s no need to add additional modules to your Prebid.js setup—our enrichment process works seamlessly with your current configuration, ensuring higher returns without extra effort.

Improve ad quality, not just revenue.

User experience matters, and not all ads are created equal. RevenuX focuses not only on boosting revenue, but also on improving the quality and relevance of the ads served on your site. By displaying ads that are less intrusive and more aligned with user interests, you can reduce ad fatigue and increase engagement, leading to higher click-through rates and more satisfied visitors.

Managing ad operations is easier with RevenuX’s centralized dashboard for integration errors.
